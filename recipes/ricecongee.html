Rice Congee

Description

When I was a kid, my mum would call this porridge. When I moved to a Western country, this caused much confusion between me and my new Australian classmates.

This is a great simple meal for if you ever feel sick and need something easy on the stomach and throat.

NOTE: you will need a rice cooker for this recipe.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups rice
  • 250g pork or chicken mince
  • 2 tsp salt

Steps

  1. Wash the rice and put it in the rice cooker. Add water up to the porridge line. If there are no markings on your rice cooker, add 4 parts water for every part rice.
  2. Break the raw mince into small chunks and add it to the rice.
  3. Add the salt to the pot.
  4. Press the 'Porridge' button on the rice cooker.
  5. After it has finished cooking, taste for salt and serve.
