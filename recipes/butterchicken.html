Butter Chicken

Description

This is the recipe for Butter Chicken, with credit going to RecipeTinEats.

Thanks to Nagi, a true Aussie hero.

Ingredients

Marinade

  • 1/2 cup plain yoghurt
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 2 tsp garam masala
  • tsp chili powder or cayenne pepper powder
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 knob of ginger
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 750g chicking thigh fillet, cut into bite-sized pieces

Curry

  • 2 tbsp ghee
  • 1 cup tomato passata
  • 1 cup heavy/thickened cream
    • <1i>1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 1/4 tsp salt

To serve - choose:

  • White rice
  • Garlic naan bread

Instructions

  1. Blitz marinade ingredients bar chicken together until smooth.
  2. Pour marinade over chicken in a bowl, and cover with aluminium foil. Refrigerate for at least 3 hours, and up to 24 hours.
  3. Heat ghee over high heat in a large fry pan. Take the chicken out of the marinade, but do not shake the marinade off of the chicken.
  4. Place chicken in the fry pan and cook for around 3 minutes, or until the chicken is white all over.
  5. Sauce: Add the tomato passata, cream, sugar and salt to the fry pan. Also add any remaining marinade in the bowl. Turn down to low and simmer for 20 mintues. Do a tast test for salt.
  6. Serve with rice and naan bread.
