Beef Vindaloo

Description

This is for Fischer. I hope you enjoy it!

Recipe from RecipeTinEats

Ingredients

  • 800g beef chuck
  • 1 1/2 tsp salt

Curry Paste

  • 6 tbsp Kashmiri chilli powder
  • 2 tsp coriander seeds
  • 3 tsp cumin seeds
  • 4 cloves
  • 4 cardamom pods
  • 2 tsp cinnamon powder
  • 1/2 tsp black peppercorns
  • 2 tbsp fresh ginger
  • 10 garlic cloves
  • 1 tsp brown sugar
  • 1 tsp fenugreek seeds
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  • 7 tbsp white vinegar
  • 2 cups water

Curry Sauce

  • 3 tbsp ghee
  • 1 onion
  • 2 tsp ginger
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 1 1/2 tsp black mustard seeds
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 10 curry leaves
  • 2 cups beef stock

Serve

  • 2 tbsp coriander leaves
  • Rice
  • Garlic naan

Steps

  1. Toss the beef in the salt.
  2. Check the spiciness of the kashmiri chilli and adjust if required.
  3. Blitz the curry paste ingredients in a blender with 1 cup of the water until smooth. Pour over beef. Pour remaining water into blender to clean out curry paste, and pour it again over the beef.
  4. Mix the beef in the curry pastee and marinae for two hours.
  5. Preheat oven to 190 degrees Celsius.
  6. Melt ghee in a wok over medium high heat. Cook onion, ginger and garlic until they become translucent - about 3 minutes.
  7. Add black mustard seeds then cook until onion is tinged with gold. Stir in tomato paste and cook for one minute.
  8. Add beef stock and stir. Transfer it into a jug then use a stick blender to puree until smooth. Pour back into the pot.
  9. Add beef and curry leaver, stir then bring to a boil.
  10. Cover with a lid then place in the oven for 2 hours or until beef is fll apart tender and the sauce has darkened in colour and thickened.
  11. Garnish with coriander and serve over basmati rice and naan.
